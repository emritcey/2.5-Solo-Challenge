Cautions

While being on vacation can be lots of fun, it is important to be cautious. Here are the top things to be cautious about.

  1. Renting Motorcycles. Crashes can be fatal.
  2. Drinking tap water in certain areas.
  3. Typhoid, Malaria, and other tropical diseases.
  4. Monkeys can and will bite!
